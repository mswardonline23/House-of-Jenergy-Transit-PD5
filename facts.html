Outer Space Facts Page

  1. An asteroid about the size of a car enters Earth’s atmosphere roughly once a year – but it burns up before it reaches us. Phew!
  2. The highest mountain known to man is on an asteroid called Vesta. Measuring a whopping 22km in height, it is three times as tall as Mount Everest!
  3. If you could fly a plane to Pluto, the trip would take more than 800 years!
